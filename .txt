2. Hospital Bed Occupancy & Patient Monitoring System
Objective: IoT system tracking bed status (pressure sensors), vital signs (pulse, SpO2), with AI anomaly detection. Data streams to central PostgreSQL dashboard; network handles 100+ concurrent devices.

Tech Stack:

ESP32 + MAX30100 sensors

AI: LSTM for abnormal vitals prediction

Database: Timeseries DB (InfluxDB)

Networking: CoAP protocol, 5G fallback
